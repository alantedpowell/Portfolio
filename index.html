What can I do for you?

Superman Software

I'm passionate about the power of software. In our current technological position, software is performing humanly impossible surgeries, helping people recover lost devices, making money transfers easier, and even getting groceries delivered to your door. Software helps solve real-world problems and unites the world together. Every line of my code is dedicated to this singular mission.

Stress Less With UI/UX

My dedication to getting software to human lives drives me to create the ultimate UX with maticulous UI Design. Software is useless if it is impossible or too complicated to utitlize. This is why in each program, I pride myself on intentionally outlining how a user is expected to interact with my software. Users are less stressed when they have a good UX.

Simple. simpler. s i m p l e s t .

Abstraction is key. Programmers (such as myself) are nerds; only we need to see the darkest secrets of a program. Because of this, I make every effort to ensure that on the front-end, users of my programs or sites only see what's necessary. Let's be honest, in 2020, minimalism is chic.

Take The Show On The Go

It is critical that a program is mobile to enhance a UX, which is why I incorporate a blueprint for program responsiveness into my UI/UX design plans. Technology unites people, that doesn't mean you have to be anchored to a desk.

Testing For Perfection

Plans fail. Humans fail. Technology is no different. Testing is an essential part to any software ranging from the genesis of the program, development, post-development, pre-deployment, and post-deployment. Each program that has a commercial amount of users that I create undergoes regularly scheduled QA testing to ensure users get the best experience, anytime, anywhere, everytime, everywhere.

"Personalize" Starts With Person

Sometimes software is not something where "one size fits all" Personalization is important. Individuality is the only way we can achieve the diversity we desperately need in our worlds. Because of this, when applicable, I incorporate accesibility features for people with disabilities or complications, and a way to make my software THEIR software. UX's are improved when an application is tailored to its user.
